About McKenzie

McKenzie Deterding is 26 years old and a resident of Nebraska. She attended Kearney High School and graduated in 2017. Throughout all of high school, she was employed as a waitress often working on weekends. After graduation, she continued waitressing for a few years before accepting a job for billing and dispatching. She studied at Central Community College in Kearney during this time, earning an Associate of Applied Science Degree in Business Administration in 2020. She was inducted into the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society in 2019. She is a notary Public for the State of Nebraska. After a couple of years there, she began her new job as an administrative assistant and worked as a bartender on Tuesdays and occasionally other days. She enjoys spending time with her friends and family in her free time. She is a big music fan and has attended many concerts, including artists like Logic, G-Eazy, Def Leppard, Zach Top, Shania Twain, and Nickelback. She is proud of everything she has been able to accomplish academically, professionally, and personally. After work, she may spend time at Hotworx or the gym. She also enjoys watching a good movie or show and leans towards suspense, drama, horror, or comedy genres. She is a big fan of Legos and proudly displays them around her home. She can ski, rollerblade, and swim, and recently, she got into golf and really found her stride. She looks forward to continuing to build connections in her current industry and with others of interest.
